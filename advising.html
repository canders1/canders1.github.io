Research positions

I don't anticipate taking on any new students in Spring 2022. However, I am looking for students for Summer 2022. If you are a Wellesley student interested in computational approaches to language, please reach out!

I generally look for students who have taken at least one CS class and at least one Linguistics class. However, if you have substantial experience in just one of these fields, feel free to reach out as well.

Independent Study

Because I have been contacted by so many students interested in natural language processing, I am going to teach a reading group as an independent study this spring (2022). We will read papers relevant to my research interests, and meet weekly to discuss them.

This group is open to any students who have taken CS111 and CS230. However, if you are an advanced Cognitive and Linguistic Sciences student who has not taken CS230, I would also love to have you in the group!
