Projects

Fulbright Canada

I'm currently a Fulbright fellow, researching the role of technology in First Nations language revitalization.

I'm hosted by Dr. Leslie Saxon at University of Victoria and Dr. Jessica Coon at McGill University.

I'm interviewing language activists, linguists, and First Nations community members about their beliefs about the relationship between technology and language revitalization.

Zapotec Linguistics
