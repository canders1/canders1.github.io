Welcome

I am third year PhD student in the Linguistics department at UMass Amherst. I graduated from Swarthmore College in 2014 with a major in Linguistics and a minor in Computer Science. My research explores the semantics of natural language from a variety of methodological frameworks: fieldwork on under-documented languages of North America; experimental work on perspective and temporal reference; and computational modeling.
